## üì± Common Scenarios

### 1. Smart TV - Fix Overscan (Cut-off Edges)

**Problem**: Content edges are cut off by the TV bezel

**Solution**:

1. Long-press for 3 seconds to open settings
2. Click the large **"‚àí"** button under Scale 3-5 times
3. Content should now be visible within the screen
4. Click **"Done"**

**Recommended Scale**: 85% - 95%

---

### 2. Vertical/Portrait Monitor

**Problem**: Display is rotated 90 degrees

**Solution**:

1. Press **Ctrl+Alt+S**
2. Click **"‚Ü∑ 5¬∞"** button 18 times (or press **]** key 18 times)
3. This rotates content 90 degrees
4. Use arrow keys to reposition if needed
5. Press **ESC**

**Rotation Angles**:

- 90¬∞ portrait (clockwise): Press **]** 18 times
- 270¬∞ portrait (counter-clockwise): Press **[** 18 times
- 180¬∞ upside down: Press **]** 36 times

---

### 3. Content Too Small

**Problem**: Content appears too small on large display

**Solution**:

1. Open settings (Ctrl+Alt+S or long-press)
2. Click the large **"+"** button under Scale
3. Continue until comfortable (recommended max: 150%)
4. Click **"Done"**

---

### 4. Content Offset/Not Centered

**Problem**: Content is shifted to one side

**Solution**:

**Method 1 - Drag** (Easiest):

1. Open settings
2. Click and drag anywhere to reposition
3. Release when centered
4. Click **"Done"**

**Method 2 - Arrow Keys** (Precise):

1. Open settings (Ctrl+Alt+S)
2. Use **Arrow Keys** to move content
3. Each press moves 10 pixels
4. Press **ESC** when done

**Method 3 - Buttons**:

1. Open settings
2. Use the directional arrow buttons (‚Üë ‚Üì ‚Üê ‚Üí)
3. Click **"Done"**

---

### 5. Multiple Monitors (Video Wall)

**Problem**: Need different settings for each monitor

**Setup**:
Each monitor/browser has its own settings. Configure each independently:

**Monitor 1 (Left)**:

- Scale: 95%
- Position: Shift right slightly
- Rotation: 0¬∞

**Monitor 2 (Center)**:

- Scale: 100%
- Position: Centered (0, 0)
- Rotation: 0¬∞

**Monitor 3 (Right)**:

- Scale: 95%
- Position: Shift left slightly
- Rotation: 0¬∞

---

### 6. Messed Up Settings - Need to Reset

**Problem**: Display looks wrong and you want to start over

**Solution**:

1. Open settings (Ctrl+Alt+S or long-press)
2. Click the yellow **"Reset"** button
3. All settings return to default (100% scale, centered, 0¬∞ rotation)
4. Click **"Done"**

**Alternative - Keyboard**:

1. Press **Ctrl+Alt+S**
2. Press **R** key
3. Press **ESC**

## üíæ Settings Storage

- ‚úÖ Settings **automatically save** when you make changes
- ‚úÖ Settings **persist** after page reload
- ‚úÖ Each browser/device has **independent settings**
- ‚ùå Settings are **lost** if you clear browser data
- ‚ùå Private/Incognito mode **won't save** settings

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Settings Won't Open

- Try alternate method (keyboard if touch doesn't work)
- Refresh the page
- Check console for errors (F12)

### Settings Not Saving

- Ensure not in private/incognito mode
- Check browser storage permissions
- Try a different browser

### Content Looks Distorted

- Press **R** to reset all settings
- Start over with small adjustments
- Don't exceed 150% scale for best quality

### Long-Press Not Working

- Ensure you're holding for full 3 seconds
- Try pressing in center of screen
- Use keyboard shortcut instead
